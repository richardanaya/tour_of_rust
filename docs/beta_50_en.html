Whew, congrats for making it through! I know it's alot to take in but you are well under way to becoming a Rustacean. Hopefully it's clear how Rust as a language aims to solve many of these common challenges in systems programming:

  • Unintentional modification of resources
  • Forgetting to deconstruct resources
  • Resources accidently being deconstructed twice
  • Using resources after they have been deconstructed
  • Data races caused by writing to resources while others are reading from resources
  • Modifying lists while iterating through them
